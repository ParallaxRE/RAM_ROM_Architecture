## 🇺🇸 Notes
ROM is a type of memory where data is permanently stored. The data inside is not lost when the computer is turned off (Non-volatile).

### Key Highlights:
* **Applications:** BIOS/Firmware storage and complex mathematical tables (Look-up Tables).
* **Function Generator:** ROM can be combined with a DAC (Digital-to-Analog Converter) to generate sine or triangle waveforms.
* **Advantage:** Instead of forcing the CPU to perform heavy mathematical calculations, reading pre-calculated data from ROM is significantly faster.
